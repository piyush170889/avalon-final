Corporate Social Responsibility

The Panchshil Foundation fosters a strong sense of community, belonging and ownership. We believe that better communities are built when people lead better lives. 

Better lives are built around basic needs and necessities. Education, health, sports, art and culture, and the environment around us are integral to building and leading better lives. In these dimensions, we focus on the individual and the community alike.

Panchshil strongly believes in the adage that charity begins at home. Our employees are a part of our family and they are the first to benefit from our social initiatives. One of our core initiatives is the facilitation of education for all and in keeping with that, Panchshil sponsors the tuition fees for all of our employees’ children from class I until XII. 

We strive to create sustainable social projects and make maximum impact in bettering lives. We shoulder the responsibility of being accountable and transparent to the community such that, with our endeavours, we may set benchmarks in all our social initiatives and be a source of pride for all stakeholders.
