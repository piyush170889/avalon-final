Fun-filled activities amidst wilderness and serenity make the Diamond Parks a perfect venue for Kids Camp this summer. Let your child be in the company of nature under our vigilant supervision as we follow international safety standards and are certified by *European Rope Course Association (ERCA). Are Safety Rangers, Safety Marshals, Women Instructors and Security Guards are there to help your child learn and enjoy the activities at the Commando Camp.
