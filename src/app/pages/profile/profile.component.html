Company Profile

Established in 2002, Panchshil Realty is one of India's finest luxury real estate developers. Renowned for its leadership and excellence in real estate development, Panchshil has successfully delivered over 23 million sq. ft. of prime real estate, with 20 million sq. ft. under development across multi-asset classes.

Panchshil's portfolio spans across high-end residential developments, IT parks, built-to-suit office spaces, special economic zones (SEZ), hotels, convention centres and luxury retail malls. The company’s developments comprise of key landmarks of tomorrow's Pune, such as India’s first internationally branded residences yoopune by Philippe Starck and John Hitchcox, and the first Trump branded residences – Trump Towers Pune, EON Free Zone in Kharadi - a 4.5 million sq. ft. IT & IT-eS SEZ spread over 57 acres, the International Convention Centre - South Asia's largest trade and convention centre, World Trade Center Pune, and an award-winning built-to-suit office campus for Cummins India amongst others.

With global partners like YOO, Trump Organization, JW Marriott, The Ritz-Carlton, Hilton, Oakwood and World Trade Center Association amongst others, Panchshil has played a significant role in putting Pune on the world map and added to its growing global identity.

Recipient of multiple awards in luxury homes and office space development, Panchshil is synonymous with premium finish, intelligent floor layouts, innovative solutions, high-quality execution, and after-sales maintenance.
